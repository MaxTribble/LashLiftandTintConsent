Lash Lift and Tint Consent Form





The lash lift technique is designed to give you longer-looking lashes without need for lash extensions. It will look as if you used a really good lash curler, only this lift lasts for 6 to 9 weeks! Lashes replace themselves every 6 to 9 weeks. I'll apply a black lash tint which really makes your lashes look more prominent. The procedure takes about 30 to 40 minutes to complete. It really is an easy way to instantly brighten and open up the eye area! Lash lift will stay lifted for 6 to 9 weeks.

On occasion some lashes are resistant, it is very difficult and sometimes not possible to sustain the curl with lashes that have a strong cowlick or slanted growth pattern. Also, growth pattern due to plastic surgery in the eye area are often not able to be corrected.
